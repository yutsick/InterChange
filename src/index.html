About Us

Portfolio Buyer is a mergers and acquisitions consulting firm that focuses on the merchant services industry. Our buyer network concentrates on purchasing merchant portfolios and residual streams. Our objective is to provide you with personalized, professional service and the maximum valuation for your portfolio. We will evaluate your situation and match you with a buyer in our network that best meets your business needs and revenue goals.

Founded in 2015, Portfolio Buyer has closed on over $75 million in residuals. Our platform ensures transparency in the auction process, allowing sellers to monitor bids and key portfolio metrics in real-time. We specialize in various portfolio categories, including low-risk, eCommerce, and brick-and-mortar portfolios, to meet diverse client needs.
